Origin Story

From the
Ground Up.

2019

The First Seed

AGGRO started as a field experiment on a 5-hectare Arabica farm in Toraja. Founder Rafi Adinugroho deployed his first DIY soil sensor array — cobbled together from Arduino boards and repurposed irrigation pipes — to track the exact moisture thresholds that produced his best cherries.

2021

The Platform Grows

After sharing results at the Indonesian Specialty Coffee Association summit, 40 farmers requested access. The homegrown system became a real platform — AGGRO v1.0. A seed funding round from Nusantara Ventures followed six months later.

2023

Scale & Partnership

AGGRO partnered with three national coffee cooperatives and integrated drone-assisted NDVI mapping into the advisory workflow. Cup score improvements averaging 4.2 points were recorded across partner farms in their first full season.

Now

400+ Farms, One Mission

Today AGGRO serves over 400 clients across Sumatra, Java, Flores, and Sulawesi — with a team of 18 agronomists, 4 data scientists, and one very well-calibrated AI.

What We Stand For

Our
Core Values.

Everything we build, advise, and measure traces back to these four principles. They are not posters on a wall — they are encoded into our platform's logic and our agronomists' field manuals.

01

Soil First

Great coffee starts underground. We never recommend a single spray, watering, or fertilizer application without verified soil data. The earth's voice comes before our advice.

02

Real Data, Real Time

Intuition has its place — but we augment it with sensors, satellites, and machine learning. Every insight on our platform is timestamped, traceable, and peer-reviewed by our agronomy team.

03

Cup Quality is Sacred

Yield without flavor is a loss. We optimize for cup score alongside volume, because specialty coffee farmers deserve to command specialty prices — and that starts with protecting the cherry.

04

Farmer Partnership

We are not a software company that visits farms. We are farming advisors who built software. That distinction shapes every interaction, every training session, and every product decision we make.
